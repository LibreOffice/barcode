============= IMPORTANT NOTES =============

Thank you for choosing Barcode!

This extension will become active after you restart the application. If you have the quickstarter running, it should also be closed during the restart.

This is an example extension created with EuroOffice Extension Creator (by MultiRacio Ltd.).

If you have any questions visit https://github.com/LibreOffice/barcode/
